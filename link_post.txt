⚽ Built something fun this weekend — FIFA World Cup 2026 Fixture Tracker 🏆

A side project I put together for myself (and fellow Bangladeshi football fans) to track all 104 matches of the 2026 World Cup in Bangladesh Standard Time (UTC+6).

🌐 Live: https://fifa26bd.vercel.app/
💻 GitHub: https://github.com/Hafiz-Sakib/fifa-wc-2026

✨ What's inside:

→ Full fixture browser — by team or by date
→ Interactive Score Predictor with animated football pitch
→ World Cup Winner Predictor (pick your champion from all 48 teams)
→ Team Squads — full 26-man rosters with clubs & positions
→ Countdown timer to kick-off
→ World Cup Quiz — 20 questions with streaks & timers
→ PDF fixture sheet download (BST times)
→ Upcoming match ticker, marquee, past champions & more

🛠 Stack: React 18 · Vite · Tailwind CSS · React Router · Lucide Icons

Nothing commercial — just a passion-driven build I enjoyed making.
If you're a football fan, give it a spin and let me know what you think! 🇧🇩⚽
